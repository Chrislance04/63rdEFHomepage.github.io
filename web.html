63rd Expeditionary Fleet

About Us

We are the 63rd Expeditionary Fleet, a Solar Auxilia and Astartes based Milsim. Our setting is during the late Great Crusade and early Horus Heresy. We are based in the United States and are currently recruiting members to fill out our Auxilia ranks. Our goal is to provide a lore-immersive experience following the story of the 63rd Expeditionary Unit led by Primarch Horus Lupercal.

What We Offer

63rd Solar Auxilia Cohort

The 63rd Solar Auxilia Cohort was a military unit of the Imperium of Man during the Great Crusade and the Horus Heresy. Composed of regular humans equipped with advanced technology and trained for warfare, they fought alongside the Sons of Horus in the service of the Warmaster.

Sons of Horus

The Sons of Horus, originally known as the Luna Wolves, were one of the original twenty Space Marine Legions created by the Emperor of Mankind during the Great Crusade. Led by the Warmaster Horus Lupercal, they were renowned for their ferocity and tactical prowess in battle.
